BAVST Winter Kids Waterproof Gloves

Keep your kids warm and dry during winter adventures with BAVST waterproof gloves designed for comfort meets protection. These gloves are perfect for any chilly outing, whether they're playing in the snow, building a snowman, or exploring the outdoors.

Featuring a waterproof outer fabric and a soft fleece lining, these gloves provide a perfect balance of insulation and waterproofing. The snug fit and adjustable velcro closure make it easy for kids to wear them all day without them slipping off or feeling tight.
